About Super Mario

I debuted as "Jumpman" in the arcade game Donkey Kong on July 9, 1981 as a carpenter. My pet ape called Donkey Kong escaped and kidnaped my girlfriend, Pauline.

My younger brother Luigi and I are Italian-American plumbers who have to defeat creatures that have been coming from the sewers below New York City.

In Super Mario Bros for the Nintendo Entertainment System, I saved Princess Toadstool (later known as Princess Peach) of the Mushroom Kingdom from King Koopa. To save Princess Toadstool, I conquered the eight worlds of the Mushroom Kingdom by going to the castle in each to defeat a minion of King Koopa. To reach each castle, I had to battle through three sub-worlds by defeating King Koopa's henchmen. Inside the eighth castle, Ihas a final fight with King Koopa and freeed Princess Toadstool.

In Super Mario Bros. 2, Luigi and I were joined by Toad and Princess Peach. Each of use using our unique abilities (Luigi has stronger jumping ability, Toad can dig the fastest, and Peach can float), with myself being the most well-rounded.

In Super Mario Bros. 3, I quested to save the rulers of seven kingdoms from Bowser and his children, the Koopalings. I traveled across eight worlds to restore order to the Mushroom World and to rescue Princess Peach.

I wil next appear in Mario and Sonic at the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020
